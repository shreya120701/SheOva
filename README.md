An interactive period tracking app with cycle logging, predictions, calendar view, and reminders.

## Features

- **Interactive Calendar**: Visual calendar with color-coded days
- **Cycle Logging**: Click any day to log or remove period data
- **Smart Predictions**: Automatic prediction of next period based on your cycle history
- **Fertile Window**: Shows your estimated fertile days
- **Statistics**: Track current cycle day, next period, and average cycle length
- **History**: View and manage all logged periods
- **Customizable Settings**: Adjust cycle and period length
- **Browser Reminders**: Get notified 3 days before expected period

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Click "Log Period Start" to record your first period
3. The app will automatically predict future periods based on your data
4. Click any calendar day to add/remove period data
5. View your history and adjust settings as needed

## Color Legend

- **Pink**: Period days (logged)
- **Light Pink**: Predicted period days
- **Light Green**: Fertile window (ovulation period)
- **Blue Border**: Today

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Your data never leaves your device.

## Browser Compatibility

Works in all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
